Murray S. Bennett

Postdoctoral Scholar | The Ohio State University

I am a postdoctoral researcher in the Psychology Department at the Ohio State University studying cognitive processing, human-AI teams, and machine learning. My research focuses on understanding human cognition and AI features to promote efficient interaction. I leverage cognitive models and machine learning techniques to analyze information processing in both human and AI systems. My current work seeks to bridge the gap between human and AI perception to improve melanoma identification by developing methods to assess AI's internal representations and enhance confidence estimation, ultimately fostering more robust and transparent human-AI collaboration.

Research Highlights

Human-AI Teams

Investigating how cognitive processes and social judgments influence collaboration between humans and AI systems, with a focus on trust and performance.

Cognitive Modeling

Using computational models to analyze information processing in decision-making and perception, providing a quantitative understanding of human and AI behavior.

Machine Learning

Applying machine learning techniques to analyze complex data from human-AI interactions and developing methods to improve AI transparency and confidence.
